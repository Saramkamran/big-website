## The Overseas Pakistani Investor

Pakistan's diaspora — with significant communities in the UK, UAE, US, and Canada — represents one of the most important investor segments in the country's capital markets. Overseas Pakistanis remit over $25 billion annually and increasingly seek structured investment vehicles to deploy capital at home.

## Key Challenges

**Multi-Jurisdictional Tax Complexity**

Navigating tax obligations across Pakistan and a country of residence requires careful planning. Our professional services network includes A-rated and QCR-rated chartered accountant firms in Pakistan, the UK, and the US.

**Currency Risk Management**

Roshan Digital Accounts (RDA) and Roshan Equity Investment Accounts (REIA) provide convenient USD-denominated access to PSX — but currency dynamics must be considered in portfolio construction.

**Inheritance and Succession Planning**

Multi-jurisdictional estate planning requires integration of Pakistani inheritance law, international succession rules, and Islamic principles around inheritance (Mirath).
