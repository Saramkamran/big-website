## Overview

Pakistan's equity markets have staged a remarkable recovery over the past 18 months, driven by IMF program stability, declining inflation, and improving corporate earnings. The PSX KMI-30 — our primary benchmark for Shariah-compliant equities — has outperformed major regional indices in H1 2026.

## Key Themes for 2026

**Monetary Policy Easing**

The State Bank of Pakistan has entered an easing cycle, with the policy rate declining from a peak of 22% to the current 14%. This benefits rate-sensitive sectors including financials, utilities, and real estate investment trusts (REITs).

**Energy Sector Restructuring**

Ongoing circular debt resolution and the privatisation agenda are creating opportunities in Pakistan's energy sector, particularly in IPPs and downstream OMCs.

**Digital Economy Growth**

Pakistan's burgeoning tech and fintech sectors are increasingly accessible through PSX listings, offering growth exposure for long-term portfolios.

## Our View

We maintain a cautiously constructive stance on Pakistani equities, with a preference for Shariah-compliant exporters, technology companies, and consumer discretionary names that benefit from improving domestic demand.
