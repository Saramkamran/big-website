## The Green Sukuk Opportunity

Green Sukuk represent a powerful convergence of Islamic finance principles and environmental responsibility. By channelling capital into renewable energy, sustainable infrastructure, and climate-resilient projects, these instruments allow investors to pursue competitive returns while contributing to a more sustainable Pakistan.

## Market Size and Growth

Pakistan's sukuk market has grown significantly, with green sukuk issuances accelerating in recent years. The government's commitment to achieving 60% renewable energy by 2030 has created a robust pipeline of green infrastructure projects requiring Shariah-compliant financing.
