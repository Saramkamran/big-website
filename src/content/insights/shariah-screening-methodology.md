## Our Screening Framework

At BIG, every investment undergoes a rigorous two-stage screening process before inclusion in client portfolios:

**Stage 1 — Business Activity Screen**

We exclude companies whose primary business involves prohibited activities under Islamic law, including conventional banking, insurance, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, entertainment (adult), and pork-related products.

**Stage 2 — Financial Ratio Screen**

We apply quantitative screens to assess a company's financial structure, excluding those with excessive leverage, liquid asset ratios, or interest-bearing income that exceeds Shariah-prescribed thresholds.

## Independent Oversight

Our screening is conducted in partnership with a certified Shariah supervisory board, providing independent oversight and ensuring our methodology aligns with AAOIFI and international Islamic finance standards.

## Ongoing Monitoring

Shariah compliance is not a one-time check — it requires continuous monitoring. We conduct quarterly reviews of all portfolio positions and notify clients of any compliance changes that require portfolio adjustments.
